Plot Summary

Baahubali: The Beginning introduces the story of Shivudu, a young man raised in a village who discovers that he is the son of Baahubali, a legendary warrior. As he learns about his heritage, he embarks on a journey to reclaim his birthright. The film showcases the battle between Shivudu and his cousin Bhallaladeva, who has usurped the throne of Mahishmati. Shivudu’s discovery of his father’s legacy sets the stage for a great conflict. Baahubali: The Conclusion reveals the past, including Baahubali’s rise to power and betrayal by his brother Bhallaladeva. It focuses on Shivudu’s journey to avenge his father’s death, reclaim the throne, and free Mahishmati from tyranny. In the end, Shivudu fulfills his destiny as the rightful king, defeating Bhallaladeva in a dramatic battle.
